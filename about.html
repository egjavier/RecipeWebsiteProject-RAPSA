Welcome to RAPSA!


I'm Eunice, a passionate food lover and aspiring programmer. This website is a labor of love where I've curated a collection of my favorite Filipino food recipes from talented Filipino chefs and cooks.

While my journey into programming is just beginning, I wanted to combine my love for Filipino cuisine and my desire to learn something new. So, I created this platform to share these delectable recipes with fellow food enthusiasts like you.

You might notice that instead of my picture, I've chosen to showcase the adorable faces of my beloved dogs. They bring joy to my life, and I hope their presence adds a touch of warmth to your experience here.

Feel free to explore the website and try out the recipes yourself. Whether you're a seasoned cook or a beginner, I believe there's something for everyone here. Cooking is an art that brings people together, and I hope these recipes foster a sense of connection and delight in your kitchen.

Thank you for joining me on this exciting journey of culinary exploration and programming. If you have any questions or feedback, please don't hesitate to REACH OUT. Enjoy the flavors, enjoy the company, and most importantly, enjoy the process!


Happy cooking,
Eunice
