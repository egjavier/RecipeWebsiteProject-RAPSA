MONGGO

Enjoy the comforting simplicity of Monggo, a hearty Filipino dish featuring mung beans simmered to perfection with a medley of vegetables, meat, and aromatic spices—a nourishing bowl of warmth and flavor.

LECHON KAWALI

Crispy, succulent, and oh-so-satisfying, Lechon Kawali is a Filipino delight—deep-fried pork belly that delivers a perfect combination of crunchy skin and tender meat in every bite.

PALABOK

A classic Filipino noodle dish featuring thin rice noodles topped with a savory shrimp sauce, crushed pork cracklings, smoked fish, and an array of delightful toppings—a delicious medley of textures and tastes.
