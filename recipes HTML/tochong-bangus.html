TOCHONG BANGUS

Kuya Fern's Cooking


INGREDIENTS:
  • oil
  • 270g tofu sliced into cubes
  • 1 1/4 kg milkfish (bangus)
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 chopped onions
  • 8 cloves chopped garlic
  • Ginger (sliced)
  • 2 chopped red tomatoes
  • 100g strained salted black beans (tausi)
  • 80g mashed tahure (fermented bean curd)
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 cups water (add more as needed)
  • 3-4 tbsp light brown sugar (depending on preferred sweetness)
  • 1 1/2 tbsp vinegar
  • slurry (1Tbsp cornstarch dissolved in 1Tbsp water)
    • HOW WAS IT?








INSTRUCTION:
  1. Heat the oil up for deep frying.
  2. Deep fry the tofu. Set aside.
  3. Put Bangus in a bowl, salt and mix.
  4. To the same oil, fry the fish. Once fried, Set aside.
  5. On a different pan with oil, saute onions on high flame for about 20sec.
  6. Add the garlic and continue sauteing for 10mins.
  7. Add ginger and saute for 30sec.
  8. Add tomatoes until mashed.
  9. Add the stained tausi saute for another 30sec.
  10. Add tahure and continue to saute on medium flame.
  11. Add oyster sauce and soy sauce.
  12. Once reduced, add water. Deglaze the pan.
  13. Add pepper, sugar and vinegar. Cook fro about 8 minutes or until the vinegar taste milder.
  14. Add the slurry.
  15. Once the sauce is thicker, add the fried bangus. Cook for about 3-5 minutes on low flame.
  16. Flip the fish to the other side.
  17. Add fried tofu. Mix gently and turn off the flame. Enjoy.
